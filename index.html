ROCK, PAPER, SCISSORS

To play: Click on the hand symbols. First to 5 points wins.

Rules: Rock Paper Scissors is a zero-sum game, typically played by two people using their hands and no tools. Players make hand shapes, each with a certain degree of power, ultimately leading to an outcome.

  • Rock wins against scissors.
  • Scissors win against paper.
  • Paper wins against rock.

Source: World Rock Paper Scissors Association
